Francisco J. PAGES
Dear Reader,
Over the last year, I have locked myself in a cave and blocked all exits and have gone hard to work to bring you WRITE ANOTHER ONE: a writing platform for stories, fictional/factual, to bring together the imagination of authors undiscovered and the eyes and hearts of avid story readers. A place to give body to your tales and hone your story telling skill.
This platform is my first major project and although it took me months to build, it is the conclusion of a journey of more than half a decade and the turning of a new page of my own story. Now I take off the hat of creator and put on the hat of editor; Over the coming months I will update the site regularly with new functionalities, polish, and quality improvements so the most important things is always front and center:
The Content
I hope you enjoy WRITE ANOTHER ONE and if you do (or don't): message me.
